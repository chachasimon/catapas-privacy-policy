Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This document explains how we handle data for the Catapas game.

Data Collection

Catapas does not collect or transmit any personally identifiable information from your device.

Local Storage

Game scores and settings are stored locally on your device's memory. This data is not shared with any third-party servers.

Internet Usage

The current version of the game does not require an internet connection for gameplay. Future updates may include optional online features.

User Privacy

We value your privacy. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties.

Security

Your game data is protected by the standard security features of your mobile operating system.

Policy Changes

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

Contact Information

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us through our official support channels.
